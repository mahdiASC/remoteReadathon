Scoring

Score Description
0
  • Has made little or no effort to answer the question
  • Does not demonstrate the All Star "spark"
  • Is not ready for a rigorous, six-week learning program
1
  • Made an effort to answer the question in full
  • Demonstrates strength in at least two "spark" competencies
  • Shows readiness and interest in a rigorous six-week learning program

  • Clearly demonstrates strength in more than two "spark" competencies

Things to remember:

  • These are high school kids with a wide range of educational opportunities and experiences. Try not to judge based on grammar or writing skills. Look for the spark!
  • Your score will be added to two other scores, so don't fear making the "wrong choice."
  • Grade each application individually; avoid side-by-side comparisons or "saving your 2s." If it's good, it's good!
